What drove our Q4 revenue growth compared to last year?
Q4 revenue grew 23% YoY to $8.4M. The primary drivers were enterprise tier upgrades (+$2.1M), representing 45% of new ARR, and improved customer acquisition in APAC (+$1.4M). Churn decreased 2.3pp to 4.1%, contributing $600K in retained revenue.
